About 40X.me

1. What is 40X.me?
A private membership club for investors introducing investment opportunities in companies with a return of investment of minimum 40 times within 4 year (40X ROI), based on our detailed review. Only emerging companies with an expected ROIx40 in 4 years are presented.

2. What is unique with 40X.me?
We provide our investment members with exclusive access to disruptive emerging companies either from Universities and Government incubators or companies that we believe will heavily contribute to our changing world. Our platform enables FAST, FUN and FAIR decisions in the Emerging Companies we are presenting. A member finding a company interesting to analyse further presses the RESERVE button on the 40X.me system to reach an investment decision.

3. How do you attract the best Emerging Companies?
The millennials running new companies wants a FUN, FAST, FAIR communication with the investors, they do not want to go on investor roadshow for 6 months and pause the operation of the company. 40X.me offers this to them by speeding up the investment process radically.

4. What is the cost of becoming an Investment Member?
After review, an Investor becomes member with their own investment club account. There is no cost associated with becoming a member.
